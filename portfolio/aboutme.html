Who am I?

Hello there and welcome to my page! My name is Timi, and I am studying Multimedia Design and Communication at KEA in Copenhagen, Denmark. Actually, I'm just finishing my first semester! Originaly I'm from Cluj-Napoca, Romania. I am half Hungarian, so one more fluently spoken language!

In my free time I enjoy reading, watching lots and lots of movies, going to the theater, museum and anything exciting really! I also love to travel (don't we all?), but I don't have as much time for it as I would like.

So, now that you have a faint idea about me, let's talk a bit about what am I exactly doing here. By studying in this course, I'm trying to improve my coding (although only frontend, so HTML, CSS and JS), learn more about User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) and develop my skills in content creation. The last one is my favourite topic to learn about, as you get the ability to make all these cool things for others like videos and animations!
